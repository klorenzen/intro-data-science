Exercise 1.

For each of the ways that gather is collected around you, brainstorm possible data science questions that are future looking, observable, and have un-obvious solutions.
  1. Number of pictures a person posts a day on a social media site.
  2. Number of times people searched for a particular word each day over several years.
  3. The number of webpages written in every language.
  4. Answers to survey results sent out to thousands of people.
  5. Number of times a particular player tries to score and the number of times they are successful.
