So, if we only have data about people's reactions to events or other objective, observable facts, what can we tell from data? Well, other objective, observable facts about the future. This is the power of data science! It also shapes the types of questions data science can answer. A good rule of thumb is that data science questions should be future looking (What do I expect to happen?), observable (in the future, I can come back and tell if I was right or wrong), and have un-obvious solutions.
